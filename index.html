WHO ARE WE?

We are a group of dedicated and enthusiastic students who come together to learn, grow, and make a difference in our field. Our club offers a range of activities and events, such as guest speaker sessions, workshops, and competitions, to help members expand their knowledge and skills.

As a member of Cyber Peace Center, you will have access to a network of like-minded individuals, valuable resources, and opportunities to showcase your talents. Whether you are just starting out or looking to advance your career, our club is here to support you every step of the way.
